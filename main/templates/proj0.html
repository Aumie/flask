PLC Competition

         This year, I have joined the competition in robo-harvesting program which is about how much you can cut mangoes from a tree. After registering a team with 3 people, we had to join their seminar first , then when we finished the seminar period, we took the exam. They chose qualified teams from this step to join their real competition. They gave each team about 2-3 months to implement the robot, and each team had to send their progress video within the deadline.

         There was 3 rounds in the competition (16->8->3 teams, I don't sure about final round), we passed to the semi-final round, and we decided to give up because of some reason. From this event, it makes me feel so uncomfortable with my knowledge, but I will keep improving myself.
